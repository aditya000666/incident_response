Mitigation vs Remedies


• Mitigation is to minimise the damage of threat
• Remediation happens when threat is eradicated
• If compromised device on network, mitigation measure is to block the connection between compromised asset and their command and control centre.Remediation measure would be to identify the malware and clean up the machine
• Mitigation is the first step: Depending upon cyber defence, blocking, isolating or shutting down may be enough to prevent damage and give security team to eliminate the threat
• Mitigation should be part of Containment strategy: Containment strategies vary based on type of incident. Design the containment strategy based on each type of attack and take into consideration factors such as potential damage, evidence preservation, service availability and more
