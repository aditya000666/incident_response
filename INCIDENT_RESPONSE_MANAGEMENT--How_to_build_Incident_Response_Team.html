How to build Incident Response Team


• Descide what types of technical background, roles and responsibilities are required
• Assign team leader to oversee IRT efforts as well as communicate incidents and progress to executive leadership
• Determine proper IRT organisational model and required functioning hours of the team
• Create security plans, policies and procedures for variety of potential threats and incidents
• Provide IRT members with routine cyber security education and awareness training
• Conduct systemwide risk assessments
• Identify critical incident response assets, including data, business processes, technology and people
• Well-Documented asset management plan
• Implement configuration management program to ensure all software is patched
