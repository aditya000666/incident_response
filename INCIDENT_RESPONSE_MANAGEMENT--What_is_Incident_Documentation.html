What is Incident Documentation


• IR team suspect incident occurred and start recording all facts regarding incident
• Logbook serve this purpose
• Leads to more efficient and less error-prone handling
• Every step from detection to final should be documented and timestamped
• The issue tracking system contains
◇ Current status of incident
◇ Summary
◇ Indicators
◇ Incident related
◇ Action taken by handler
◇ Chain of custody
◇ Impact
◇ Contact information of involved parties
◇ List of evidence gathered
◇ Comments from incident handler
◇ Next steps
